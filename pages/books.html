Shattered Sword: The Untold Story of the Battle of Midway
Clearly intended for someone better-versed in existing narratives about the Battle of Midway, and more generally about the Pacific War, than I am, but nonetheless full of interesting tidbits. Excellent at connecting the smallest details of carrier operation to the flow of the larger battle as a whole - "for want of faster flight deck elevators, the Empire was lost". Also convinced me that man was neither meant to fly through the air nor sail on the sea, and certainly not to do both at the same time, as naval aviators do.
